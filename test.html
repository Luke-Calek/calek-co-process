Testing Focus

To create amazing experiences focus on testing to discovrying : Brilliant Basics, Iconic Moves & Extended Experience.

  • Desirability (user) - does this idea solve the users needs? Is it desirable for the user?

  • Viability (business) - does this idea match the business plan for investment or operation? Is it viable for the business?

  • Feasability (technical) - does this idea fit with the technical possibilites or scope? Is it feasable technically?

It is at the intersection of these three focuses (imagine a venn-diagram) that innovative experience are created. If you can find a new solution and it also relevant to the user and business â€“ you've just innovated!
