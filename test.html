Anaytics

Analytics tools are a useful secondary type of testing that will help you track quantitativly user's behaviour.

Popular analytics tools Google Analytics

Web analytics service from Google that tracks and reports on website traffic.

Plausible

Cookie-free simple alternative to Google Analytics.

HotJar

Monitor behaviour via heatmaps and recordings or collect feedback via intercept surveys.

Further reading: Google Analytics Academy

Free online courses for Google Analytics. While these are Google specific, the concepts will apply to all tracking software.
