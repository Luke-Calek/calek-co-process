Overview

The principles outlined on this website transcend the different UX processes (see next section) and are core modes of working that a UX designer will do to create an experience.

Principles:

  • Understand
  • Ideate
  • Test
  • Implement

These principles, and the methods within, are often presented in a linear left-to-right journey, but for a UX project to be successful, these principles and methods should be repeated often as part of a cycle of creation. Once the solution is created, smaller cycles should be continually used to maintain and evolve the product, service or space to meet changing user needs.
