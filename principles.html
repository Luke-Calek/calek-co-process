Principles

← Homepage

The principles outlined on this website transcend different design processes (see next section) and are core modes of working that a XD will use to create an experience.

Principles:

  • Understand
  • Ideate
  • Test
  • Deliver

These principles, and the methods within, are often presented in a linear left-to-right journey, but for a digital product or service project to be successful, these principles and methods should be repeated regularly. Once a solution is created, smaller cycles should be continually used to maintain and evolve the product, service, or space to meet changing user needs.
