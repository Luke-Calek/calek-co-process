About this Guide

This living document is my attempt to formally document the way I work in the UX field as well as the tools and resources I try to use for most projects.

What this guide is...

  • A collection of methods and frameworks for UX design
  • Links and resources for further learning or hands-on practice
  • Mostly built from working knowledge collected over the last 10 years. Very few of the things documented on this website are my original thinking, so where possible I've cited sources.

What this guide is not...

  • Detailed step-by-step instruction and training on how to be a good UX designer
  • Substitute for formal design training or education
  • Always correct - there will be mistakes. Please email luke@calek.co if you find any.


Created by Luke Caish-Sadlek as a method of documenting processes and sharing knowledge.
